Mammoth
-------------
    This pre-historic behemoth will not be aggressive unless you poke it, or, well, try to kill it.
    Its huge tusks can crush an enemy, regardless if they are wearing armor.
    Abilities: Stampede

Sabertooth Tiger
-------------
    This predatory beast is fast, feracious, and will attack anything it deems tasty in sight.
    Abilities: Barrel Charge
