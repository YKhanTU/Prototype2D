Commander's Aura
-------------
    Within a certain radius of the Ruler, an Entity is given this buff with the
    added affect increasing the player's Health, Damage Output, and Movement Speed.

Homestead Euphoria
-------------
    Your Nation is enriched with bountiful food and water, liberated from disease and hardship. This realization
    has spread to your mind and has made your citizens very happy. This increases their Health and Movement Speed.

Bleeding
-------------
    Creates a bleeding particle effect off of the bleeding Entity once damaged.
    Bleeding lasts for 3-5 seconds and is done by chance based off of opponent's weapon penetration factor.

Starvation
-------------
    Makes the Entity slower than usual and slowly reduces their health over a long period of time.
