Time Traveler
-------------
    An young man that visits your city's gates and gifts you a new piece of technology.
    He wears technological gadgets around his 'suit' and does not fit in well with the
    surrounding population

G-Man (Shout out to Garry's Mod)
-------------
    An old, mysterious man carrying a briefcase that suddenly appears randomly in your view.
    He disappears once the player moves the screen, mouse, or after a short period of time.
    
Shade Glider
-------------
    This scary reaper comes during the night time to attack your city and plague them with mayhem.
    His abilities include lifting up pieces of the ground and throwing it towards his enemies.
    Anyone he touches or goes near his aura, they are given the 'Madmen Disease', that makes them
    easily immune to insanity.
